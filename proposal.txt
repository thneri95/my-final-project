# PROJECT PROPOSAL: FINANCE LIFE HUB

## OVERVIEW  
Finance Life Hub (FLH) is a centralized personal finance dashboard designed to help users manage their money in one place. The app addresses a common problem: people often track expenses, savings goals, bills, and financial information across multiple apps or notebooks, making it difficult to stay organized and make informed decisions.  
FLH provides a customizable and user-friendly workspace where users can monitor income and expenses, manage monthly budgets, track savings progress, and view key financial insights in real time. The goal is to simplify daily financial management and support better financial habits.

---

## TARGET AUDIENCE  
This app is designed for:
- College students managing limited monthly budgets  
- Young professionals building savings and controlling spending  
- Individuals or families who want a simple all-in-one finance dashboard  
- Users who want better financial visibility without complex accounting software  

---

## MAJOR FUNCTIONS

### 1) Budget & Expense Tracker  
- Add, edit, and delete income/expense transactions  
- Organize transactions by category (Food, Transport, Rent, Utilities, etc.)  
- View monthly totals and category-level spending breakdown  

### 2) Savings Goals Module  
- Create personalized savings goals (e.g., Emergency Fund, Tuition, Travel)  
- Set target amount and deadline  
- Visualize progress through progress bars and milestone indicators  

### 3) Bills & Reminders Module  
- Track recurring bills and due dates  
- Display upcoming payments in dashboard alerts  
- Mark bills as paid to maintain payment history  

### 4) Debt Payoff Calculator  
- Input debt amount, interest rate, and monthly payment  
- Estimate payoff duration and total interest paid  
- Compare payoff scenarios using different payment values  

### 5) Financial Snapshot Dashboard  
- Show key metrics: total income, total expenses, remaining balance, savings progress  
- Display monthly trend charts for spending behavior  
- Provide quick financial insights (e.g., highest spending category)  

### 6) Quick Links & Notes Panel  
- Save links to bank portals, scholarship pages, loan websites, or budgeting resources  
- Keep short notes for financial plans, reminders, or ideas  

---

## WIREFRAME (PLANNED SCREENS)  
- **Dashboard (Home):** summary cards, alerts, charts, quick actions  
- **Transactions Page:** list + filters + add/edit form  
- **Savings Goals Page:** goals list, create goal form, progress view  
- **Bills Page:** due-date panel, paid/unpaid status management  
- **Tools Page:** debt calculator + quick links + notes  
- **Settings Page:** categories, theme, profile preferences  

---

## EXTERNAL DATA  
Finance Life Hub will use the following APIs:

1. **Alpha Vantage API**  
   - For basic market widgets (stocks/forex overview)  
   - https://www.alphavantage.co/

2. **ExchangeRate-API** (optional/alternative)  
   - For currency conversion support  
   - https://www.exchangerate-api.com/

3. **NewsAPI** (optional enhancement)  
   - For financial/economic headline cards on dashboard  
   - https://newsapi.org/

> Note: Core app features (budgeting, goals, bills, debt calculator) will work independently of external APIs to ensure reliability.
