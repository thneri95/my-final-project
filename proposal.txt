
Overview 
My Finance Life Hub Application is a centralized personal finance dashboard designed to help users manage their money in one place;
The app addresses a common problem: people often track expenses, savings goals, bills, and financial information across multiple apps or notebooks, making it difficult to stay organized and make informed decisions;

My Application provides a customizable and user-friendly workspace where users can monitor income and expenses, manage monthly budgets, track savings progress, and view key financial insights in real time. The goal is to simplify daily financial management and support better financial habits;

KEY OBJECTIVES
The strategic goals for Finance Life Hub include:
Simplify daily financial management by consolidating fragmented data into a single dashboard;
Promote financial literacy and better habits through real-time insights and progress tracking;
Ensure system reliability by prioritizing core offline functionality over external dependencies;


Target Audience 
This app is designed for:
College students managing limited monthly budgets;  
Young professionals building savings and controlling spending; 
Individuals or families who want a simple all-in-one finance dashboard;  
Users who want better financial visibility without complex accounting software;

Major Functions:

1) Budget & Expense Tracker  
Add, edit, and delete income/expense transactions;  
Organize transactions by category (Food, Transport, Rent, Utilities); 
View monthly totals and category-level spending breakdown;  

2) Savings Goals Module  
Create personalized savings goals ( Emergency Fund, Tuition, Travel);  
Set target amount and deadline;  
Visualize progress through progress bars and milestone indicators;  

3) Bills & Reminders Module  
Track recurring bills and due dates;  
Display upcoming payments in dashboard alerts;  
Mark bills as paid to maintain payment history;  


4) Debt Payoff Calculator  
Input debt amount, interest rate, and monthly payment;  
Estimate payoff duration and total interest paid;  
Compare payoff scenarios using different payment values;  

5) Financial Snapshot Dashboard  
Show key metrics: total income, total expenses, remaining balance, savings progress;  
Display monthly trend charts for spending behavior;  
Provide quick financial insights (highest spending category);  

6) Quick Links & Notes Panel  
Save links to bank portals, scholarship pages, loan websites, or budgeting resources;  
Keep short notes for financial plans, reminders, or ideas;  


Wireframe
Dashboard (Home): summary cards, alerts, charts, quick actions;  
Transactions Page: list + filters + add/edit form;  
Savings Goals Page: goals list, create goal form, progress view;  
Bills Page: due-date panel, paid/unpaid status management;  
Tools Page: debt calculator + quick links + notes;  
Settings Page: categories, profile preferences;  

External Data 
Finance Life Hub will use the following APIs:

1. Alpha Vantage API  
   For basic market widgets (stocks/forex overview)  
   https://www.alphavantage.co/

2. ExchangeRate-API   
   For currency conversion support  
   https://www.exchangerate-api.com/

3. NewsAPI
   For financial/economic headline cards on dashboard  
   https://newsapi.org/

Obs/ Note: Core app features (budgeting, goals, bills, debt calculator) will work independently of external APIs to ensure reliability;

Challenge expected

The primary challenge lies in the technical complexity of integrating disparate financial APIs while maintaining a clean, high-performance data flow between modules;
To mitigate this risk and ensure reliability, the project will follow a phased roadmap:
Mark 01: Stable MVP, Focus on core modules including transactions, budgets, savings, and local storage;
Phase 02: Advanced Features, Integration of external APIs and UI/UX polish;
